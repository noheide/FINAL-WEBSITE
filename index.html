About Me

Who is Noah Heide anyways?

I would love to connect.
My passion for film making started on a 6 hour bus ride to a volleyball championship. The popular song by One Republic “I Ain’t Worried” was playing on the speaker, and I thought there was nothing better to do then start a music video right there in the bus with my iPhone. Boy was I right. The video was posted everywhere on my high school’s socials.

Hey! I’m Noah Heide, a 20 year old filmmaker chasing my passion based out of Kitchener, Ontario. Now that you know where it all started, it’s time to tell you a bit about myself. I am a student currently enrolled in the Global Business and Digital Arts program at the University of Waterloo. The first camera I bought was the Sony A6400, and the first thing I did with it was a 10 day trip to Halifax. I stayed at 5 different Airbnb’s for free, in exchange for free professional photography of the property to use for their listing. If you can’t tell already, I love to travel. In 2024 during my gap year, I bought a DJI mini 2 se drone and went to Spain and Portugal, capturing stunning views that I wouldn’t otherwise get to see. Some of these shots can be found in my “Chasing the Now” short film, showcased above. When I’m not holding a camera, editing, planning, and traveling, I am playing guitar, running, or spending time with friends. I believe in always doing something to better yourself, and staying busy with those things.

Every film I create can be customized and adapted to your dreams.
